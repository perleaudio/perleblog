Privacy Policy for Perle Audio

Effective Date: 12/07/2023

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how Perle Audio (“we”, “us”, or “our”) collects, uses, and discloses your information when you visit or use our website and audio content (the “Service”).

2. Information We Collect

We collect various types of information when you use the Service, including:

Personal Information:

  • Contact information: Name, email address, phone number
  • Usage data: Information about how you use the Service, such as the pages you visit, the links you click, and the audio content you listen to
  • Device information: Information about your device, such as your IP address, browser type, operating system, and device identifiers
  • Cookies and similar tracking technologies: Information collected through cookies and similar tracking technologies, such as your preferences and browsing activities

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide and operate the Service: We use your information to provide you with the Service, such as delivering audio content, responding to your inquiries, and improving the overall user experience.
  • Personalize your experience: We use your information to personalize your experience with the Service, such as recommending audio content that you may be interested in.
  • Send you marketing and promotional communications: We may use your information to send you marketing and promotional communications, such as email newsletters or notifications about new features and content.
  • Analyze and improve the Service: We use your information to analyze how the Service is used and to make improvements.
  • Comply with legal obligations: We may use your information to comply with legal obligations, such as responding to requests from government agencies.

4. Sharing Your Information

We may share your information with the following third parties:

  • Service Providers: We may share your information with service providers who help us operate the Service, such as web hosting providers, analytics providers, and marketing providers.
  • Business Partners: We may share your information with business partners, such as affiliate partners, to offer you relevant products and services.
  • Law enforcement or other government agencies: We may disclose your information to law enforcement or other government agencies if we are required to do so by law.

5. Your Choices

You have choices regarding the use and disclosure of your information:

  • Cookies: You can control the use of cookies by your browser settings.
  • Marketing communications: You can unsubscribe from our marketing communications by clicking the “unsubscribe” link in any marketing email you receive from us.

6. Data Retention

We will retain your information for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this Privacy Policy, unless a longer retention period is required or permitted by law.

7. Security of Your Information

We take reasonable security measures to protect your information from unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, or destruction. However, no website or Internet transmission is completely secure. We cannot guarantee the security of your information.

8. International Users

The Service is hosted in the United States. If you access the Service from outside the United States, your information may be transferred to and processed in the United States.

9. Changes to this Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page. You are advised to review this Privacy Policy regularly for any changes. Changes to this Privacy Policy are effective when they are posted on this page.
